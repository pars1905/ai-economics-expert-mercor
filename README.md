## 📌 Project Overview

Selected as a **Finance & Economics Domain Expert** on Mercor's professional AI trainer network to contribute to LLM development for a confidential enterprise client. The project focused on evaluating and improving AI-generated content in finance and economics domains.

**Duration:** ~2–3 weeks (April 2026)  
**Weekly Commitment:** ~30 hours  
**Methodology:** RLHF (Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback)  
**Client:** Confidential (via Mercor)  
**Focus Areas:** Financial analysis, economic reasoning, investment evaluation, macroeconomic interpretation

---

## 🎯 Role & Responsibilities

- Evaluated AI-generated finance and economics responses using pairwise comparison (Response A vs Response B)
- Assessed outputs for **economic accuracy**, **financial reasoning quality**, and **analytical depth**
- Applied expertise in macroeconomics, investment analysis, and financial markets
- Provided structured feedback to improve model performance in economics and finance contexts
- Ensured alignment with professional economic and financial standards

## 💡 Key Insight

> Economics and finance AI evaluation requires understanding not just whether an answer is factually correct, but whether the **economic reasoning chain** is sound. A response can cite correct data while drawing fundamentally flawed macroeconomic conclusions — detecting this requires deep domain expertise.
